## Key Differentiators

### 1. 4-Year FREE Maintenance (Unique!)
This is their **biggest differentiator**. Most companies offer 1-year basic warranty. Arkahub includes:
- Monthly panel health check-up
- Professional panel cleaning
- For over 4 years — at no extra cost

This eliminates the #1 customer complaint (AMC neglect) by baking it in upfront.

### 2. Institutional Backing
- **$2M seed round** — means they have marketing budget to scale
- **Kae Capital** — known Indian VC, serious about this space
- **Antler** — global startup incubator
- This gives them credibility AND capital

### 3. In-House Expertise (Full-Stack)
- Site survey (in-house)
- Installation (in-house)
- Regulatory approvals (handled for customer)
- Not a lead aggregator — they execute

## What VSS Can Learn From Arkahub

1. **FREE maintenance for 4 years** — this is brilliant. VSS should consider a similar bundled AMC offer
2. **Multiple mounting options** — ArkaCore/AryaSky/AryaScape shows different roof solutions; VSS should develop mounting expertise for all roof types
3. **Funding credibility** — even if VSS doesn't seek VC, "backed by institutional investors" shows up in Arkahub's marketing
4. **EMI Calculator on website** — VSS should have an interactive tool on their site
5. **"Lifetime Partnership" tagline** — simple, powerful brand positioning

---

## VSS Threat Assessment
- **Threat level:** 🔴 HIGH for Bangalore residential market
- Arkahub is explicitly targeting Bangalore homeowners with a superior service bundle
- Their $2M funding means they can outspend VSS on marketing
- 4-year free maintenance is a powerful competitive weapon VSS must address
