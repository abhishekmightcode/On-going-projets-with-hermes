## 4. Operational Workflow (10 Steps)

1. **Lead Generation** — Performance marketing + referrals
2. **Qualification** — ICP fit assessment
3. **Site Inspection** — Rooftop area, direction, shadow profiles
4. **Proposal** — Custom engineering + ROI presentation
5. **Closure** — Negotiation + project sign-off
6. **Documentation** — DISCOM approvals + net metering
7. **Installation** — Precision execution (1-3 days)
8. **Testing** — Commissioning + safety audits
9. **Net Metering** — Utility grid integration
10. **Support** — AMC + performance monitoring
