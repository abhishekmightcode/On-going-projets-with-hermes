# VSS — Human Capital Architecture

> **Source:** Master Strategy Document (Section 5)  
> **Date:** May 2026

---

## Culture: High-Ownership

VSS culture is defined by **speed, profitability, and extreme ownership**. We do not hire for "roles"; we hire for "outcomes." Every team member is expected to be a self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced environment where **results are the only currency**.

---

## Role Definitions

### Social Media Manager (Content & Video Lead)

A hybrid creative-strategist role. Leads the digital brand voice across Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

**Responsibilities:**
- 10-20 static posts + 10-15 video/reel posts monthly
- On-ground content capture
- Brand voice consistency

### Business Development Senior Executive

High-impact, revenue-driven role. Owns full sales lifecycle — from lead qualification to deal closure.

**Requirements:**
- Fluent in Kannada & English
- ICP framework fluency
- CRM hygiene discipline
- Sharp focus on conversion rates

### Sales Representative (Field)

Entry-level, field-oriented role.

**Requirements:**
- Kannada, Hindi, English proficiency
- Counter visits + relationship management
- Meeting aggressive sales targets
- **Stipend:** ₹5,000 + uncapped incentives

## Employment Framework

### Probation Structure

- **Duration:** 2 months
- **Month 1:** Mentorship + creative direction provided
- **Month 2:** Independent accountability begins
- **Extension:** Up to 2 additional months if benchmarks not met

### Notice Period

- Post-confirmation: 1 month notice
- Resignation without notice: 1 month's gross salary recovery

### Special Clauses

| Clause | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **IP Ownership** | All content, strategies, creative assets → VSS property |
| **Digital Handover** | All login credentials in shared system; full handover within 48h of separation |
| **Non-Compete** | 6 months post-separation; no direct solar competitor in Bangalore; no client solicitation |
| **Code of Conduct** | Professional standards, punctuality, brand integrity |
| **At-Will (Probation)** | Either party may terminate with 7 days notice |

### First Month Performance Gate

Continuation beyond Month 1 is contingent upon management satisfaction. Employment may be concluded at end of Month 1 if standards are not met.
