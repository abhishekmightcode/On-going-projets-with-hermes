## Case Study: Samsung Experience Stores

**Location:** Malleshwaram & Koramangala, Bangalore

**Context:** High-traffic retail environment requires 100% uptime — any power disruption means lost transactions and damaged customer experience.

**Solution Deployed:**
- Luminous UPS systems (2.5 KVA & 3 KVA)
- POS system protection
- Lighting circuit backup

**Outcome:** Operational continuity maintained during grid fluctuations. Samsung's customer experience standard upheld.

All successful projects share a common thread:

1. **Consultative discovery** — Not selling products, understanding needs

3. **Execution excellence** — Fast, clean installation
4. **Life-cycle support** — AMC and follow-up
