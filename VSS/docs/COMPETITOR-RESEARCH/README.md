## 📊 Key Findings Summary

### Competitive Landscape
1. **ZunSolar** is the most aggressive national player in Bangalore — IITian branding, heavy digital marketing, 40,000+ customers
2. **Orange Energy** has the highest Google rating (5.0) among named competitors — strong testimonials, diverse segments
3. **Kamal Solar & Power Planet** have 4.9 ratings — established local players with loyal customer bases
4. **EcoSoch** differentiates on CII certification, CLEAN Energy certification, and 10MW+ installed

### Pricing Observable
- ZunSolar: ₹45,000–₹65,000/kW residential (subsidy-inclusive)
- Orange Energy: Active in residential, commercial, industrial, solar farming
- EcoSoch: 1800+ customers, 25+ team, 10MW+ installed

### Scaling Models Observed
1. **Franchise/Partner Model** — ZunSolar (75+ cities via partner network)
2. **Vertical Integration** — VSS style (design → supply → install → AMC)
3. **Aggregator Model** — Lead gen platforms (IndiaMART, Zunpulse)
4. **IoT + App-First** — ZunSolar (remote monitoring as differentiator)
5. **Specialized Niche** — Solar farming (Orange Energy), EV charging (EcoSoch)
