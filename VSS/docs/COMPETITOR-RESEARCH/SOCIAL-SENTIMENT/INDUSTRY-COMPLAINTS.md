# Solar Industry — Common Complaints & Red Flags
**Research note:** Reddit/Quora and consumer complaint sites were blocked during research. These findings are compiled from general solar industry patterns, review analysis, and competitor intelligence.

---

## Known Complaint Patterns in Solar Industry

### 1. Post-Sale Disappearance
**The complaint:** "Signed the deal, gave 50% advance. After installation, nobody answers calls."

**Why it happens:**
- Small companies close and reopen under new names
- Sales person leaves and no handover
- Company overwhelmed with installations, can't service all

**Red flag for customers:** Company with no physical office, no named team members

---

### 2. Panel Quality Deception
**The complaint:** "Promised Tier-1 panels, gave me Tier-B. System generates 20% less than neighbors."

**Why it happens:**
- Tier-1 panels (Tata, Adani, JA Solar, Longi) cost 15–20% more
- Companies substitute cheaper panels without disclosure
- "25-year warranty" is on paper only — brand may not exist in 10 years

**Red flag:** Quote that seems too low vs. market rate

---

### 3. Inverter Failure / Warranty Void
**The complaint:** "Inverter failed in 18 months. Company says warranty requires 'authorized service center' — none exist."

**Why it happens:**
- Companies use cheap inverter brands to save cost
- Warranty is conditional (requires annual AMC at extra cost)
- Imported inverter brands with no India service network

**Red flag:** Inverter brand not internationally known (e.g., generic Chinese)

---

### 4. Subsidy Fraud
**The complaint:** "Company said subsidy is included. After installation, subsidy never came. Lost ₹80,000."

**Why it happens:**
- Company shows inflated initial price, "subsidy" is just their margin
- MNRE subsidy requires proper documentation — some companies don't know process
- Customer doesn't realize subsidy is government, not company's to give

**Red flag:** "Subsidy included" without clear breakdown of MNRE amount vs. company discount

---

### 5. Net Metering Delay / Rejection
**The complaint:** "Company said net metering is easy. 4 months later, still waiting. Paying double electricity."

**Why it happens:**
- Company doesn't understand BESCOM technical requirements
- Customer's electrical setup doesn't meet grid connection standards
- Application filed incorrectly, multiple rejections

**Red flag:** Company that promises net metering without site assessment

---

### 6. AMC Expiry Shock
**The complaint:** "AMC was sold as optional. Year 2, system stops working. No AMC = no service."

**Why it happens:**
- Companies push AMC as upsell but don't emphasize necessity
- Year 1 is covered by basic warranty; Year 2+ requires AMC
- Cleaning and inverter service needed annually

**Red flag:** Company not offering clear AMC pricing before installation

---

### 7. Installation Damage
**The complaint:** "Roof started leaking after solar installation. Company says not their problem."

**Why it happens:**
- Penetrative roof mounting without proper waterproofing
- Civil work by unqualified labor
- No coordination with building's waterproofing contractor

**Red flag:** Company using daily-wage labor for civil mounting work

---

### 8. Generation Guarantee Breach
**The complaint:** "Promised 20 units/day from 5kW. Getting 12 units. Company says it's seasonal."

**Why it happens:**
- Shadow profiling not done correctly
- Panel angle wrong for Bangalore latitude
- Partial shading from new construction/tree growth

**Red flag:** Company quoting generation without doing shadow analysis

## Solar Industry Scam Patterns

### The "Free Solar" Scam
**Pattern:** Company offers "free solar, you pay only savings from electricity bill"
**Reality:** Company takes roof lease, customer gets small monthly payment, company takes rest of savings and MNRE subsidy
**What to watch:** Long-term lease agreements, transfer of subsidy to company

### The "Govt. Funded" Scam
**Pattern:** "Government is funding 100% of your solar, you pay nothing"
**Reality:** Fake MNRE scheme, customer pays full price, company pockets subsidy
**What to watch:** Anything claiming "100% government funded"

### The "BESCOM Approved" Scam
**Pattern:** Company claims BESCOM-approved vendor
**Reality:** No such formal vendor list exists — BESCOM doesn't "approve" companies
**What to watch:** Ask for MNRE empanelment certificate instead

## How to Protect VSS from These Complaints

| Complaint Type | VSS Prevention |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Disappearance | Named team members, WhatsApp contact, physical office |
| Panel quality | Only Tier-1 brands, show customer invoice of panel purchase |
| Inverter warranty | Use Tata/Adani/Fronsius inverter, explain warranty clearly |
| Subsidy fraud | Show MNRE amount separately, handle paperwork proactively |
| Net metering | Site assessment first, confirm feasibility before quoting |
| AMC neglect | AMC pitch at point of sale, not after |
| Installation damage | Use trained civil workers, waterproof properly, document with photos |
| Generation guarantee | Do shadow profiling first, quote realistic numbers |

## Red Flags for VSS to Avoid (Internal)

These are things VSS should NEVER do, because customers remember and review:
1. ❌ Promise a timeline you can't keep
2. ❌ Substitute panel brands without disclosure
3. ❌ Use generic/unknown inverter brands
4. ❌ Take full advance and then go silent
5. ❌ Skip shadow profiling and quote blindly
6. ❌ Overlook net metering feasibility in site assessment
7. ❌ Skip AMC conversation at point of sale
8. ❌ Use untrained labor for civil mounting

---

## Red Flags for VSS Sales Team to Watch (Customer Side)

These customer behaviors may indicate a bad deal:
1. 🔴 Customer wants everything in writing that can't be guaranteed (exact generation)
2. 🔴 Customer who has been burned by previous solar installer (high expectations, low trust)
3. 🔴 Customer unwilling to sign proper agreement (suggests they want to dispute later)
4. 🔴 Customer asking for cash payment / no invoice (suggests tax evasion)
5. 🔴 RWA president who wants "agency" commission (kickback situation)
