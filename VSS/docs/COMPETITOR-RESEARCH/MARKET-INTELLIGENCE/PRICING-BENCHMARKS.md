## What Determines Price?

| Factor | Lower Price | Higher Price |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| **Panel brand** | Tier-B Chinese (₹18–22/watt) | Tier-1 Indian/International (₹25–35/watt) |
| **Panel type** | Polycrystalline (old tech) | Mono PERC / TOPCon (new tech) |
| **Inverter** | Generic Chinese (₹8–12k) | Tata / SMA / Fronius (₹20–40k) |
| **Mounting** | Basic Angle Iron | Hot-dip galvanized with proper civil |
| **Wiring** | Local cables | Finolex / Polycab DC cables |
| **Monitoring** | None | IoT-based app monitoring (+₹5–10k) |
| **Installation** | Sub-contracted | In-house trained team |

## VSS Pricing Strategy

### Current VSS model (based on quotegen tools):
- On-grid system: ₹50,000–₹65,000/kW
- Off-grid system: ₹80,000–₹1,20,000/kW
- UPS B2B: Custom pricing

### Recommendations:
1. **Never compete on lowest price** — you lose margin and attract wrong customers
2. **Price based on:** Panel brand + Inverter brand + Installation quality + AMC included
3. **Always show subsidy amount separately** — makes customer feel "government is paying"
4. **Include AMC as line item** — don't hide it, show ₹2,000–₹5,000/year
5. **Financing as upsell** — 0% EMI makes ₹2 lakh system feel like ₹6,000/month

## ROI for Customer

**5 kW system in Bangalore:**
- Monthly generation: 600–750 units (avg, depending on season)
- Monthly savings: ₹4,200 – ₹7,500 (at ₹7/unit avg tariff)
- Annual savings: ₹50,000 – ₹90,000
- Payback period: 4–6 years (with subsidy), 6–8 years (without)

**This is VSS's strongest sales argument — show the math, not just the product.**
