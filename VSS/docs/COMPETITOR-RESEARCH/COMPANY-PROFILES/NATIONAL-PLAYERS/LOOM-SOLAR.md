## Business Model: Manufacturer + DTC + Dealer

Loom Solar is unique in the Indian solar market — they **manufacture their own panels and batteries** AND sell direct to consumers AND supply dealers. This vertical integration in manufacturing gives them cost control that pure-EPC companies lack.

### Manufacturing Capabilities:
1. **Solar Panels** — Panel manufacturing unit in Ferozpur, Palwal
2. **Lithium Batteries** — Battery/inverter manufacturing in Faridabad
3. **PV Inverters** — Own-brand inverters

### Distribution Channels:
1. **Direct-to-Consumer** — Website, phone, WhatsApp sales
2. **Dealer Network** — 10,000+ dealers across India
3. **Engineer Visit** — Sends engineers for site survey
4. **EMI Partners** — NBFC and bank partnerships for 0% EMI

## Sales & Marketing Strategy

### 1. "Save up to 80% on monthly electricity bills"
Clear, quantifiable value proposition on their homepage.

### 2. EMI as Primary Sales Tool
- EMI = electricity bill equivalent
- "Solar on EMI" = "pay as you save" model
- Strong NBFC and bank partnerships

### 3. Dealer Network (10,000+)
- Loom supplies panels + inverter
- Local dealer handles installation
- Loom provides training and brand support
- This is how they scale nationally without heavy EPC overhead

### 4. Instant Digital Service
- "Instant digital service through video calls"
- "Complaint resolution within 3–7 days across India"

## What VSS Can Learn From Loom Solar

1. **EMI partnerships** — VSS should establish relationships with NBFCs for 0% EMI offerings
2. **Dealer network model** — could VSS become a dealer for Loom Solar panels while also having its own brand?
3. **"Instant service through video calls"** — use WhatsApp video for quick remote troubleshooting
4. **Complaint resolution SLA** — "within 3–7 days" is a service promise; VSS should make explicit SLAs
5. **"Save up to 80%"** — very specific claim; VSS should calculate and publish its own savings claims

---

## VSS Threat Assessment
- **Threat level:** 🟡 MEDIUM in Bangalore
- Loom Solar operates in Bangalore but primarily through dealer network
- Dealer quality is variable — VSS (in-house team) can out-execute on quality
- VSS's local presence and relationship model is a differentiator vs. dealer-based Loom Solar
- However, Loom Solar's brand recognition and pricing are strong
