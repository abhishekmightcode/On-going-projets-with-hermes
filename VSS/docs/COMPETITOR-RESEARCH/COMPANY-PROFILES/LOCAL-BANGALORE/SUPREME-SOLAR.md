## What VSS Can Learn From Supreme Solar

1. **Water hardness testing** — Supreme tests water before installing. VSS could offer free water quality assessment as part of site survey
2. **Long operating history** — VSS should document and market its own years of experience
3. **Product bundling** — VSS could bundle solar with water heater (or solar + EV charging) for complete home offering
4. **IIT-B / IISc partnerships** — Supreme's engineering credibility is implicit; VSS could seek explicit certification
