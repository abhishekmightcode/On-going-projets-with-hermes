## Key Differentiators

### 1. Savings Guarantee (Money-Back Promise)
**This is their #1 differentiator.** SolarSquare claims:
- "Save up to 90% on home electricity bills"
- "India's only solar company that offers a Savings Guarantee with a money-back promise"
- "Money-back promise at Rs. 8/unit"

**What this means in practice:**
- If customer's actual per-unit savings is less than Rs. 8, SolarSquare refunds the difference
- This transfers performance risk from customer to company
- Very few solar companies do this

### 2. GoodZero™ Plan
**India's only Guaranteed Solar Savings Plan**
- Money-back promise at Rs. 8/unit
- Regular proactive maintenance visits
- ₹0 Repair & Replacement Cost
- App for real-time power generation tracking

### 3. WindPro Mount™ (IIT Bombay Approved)
- WindPro Mount™ tested for **170 kmph storms**
- "Built for India's toughest weather"
- Approved by **IIT Bombay**

This directly addresses Bangalore's occasional strong winds and monsoon conditions. It's also a strong credibility signal — IIT Bombay endorsement.

### 4. Hassle-Free Subsidy Process
- "Installation, subsidy and service — all handled directly by us. Zero middlemen."
- Company handles all MNRE subsidy paperwork
- Customer doesn't have to run around government offices

## What VSS Can Learn From SolarSquare

1. **Savings guarantee model** — VSS should consider a performance guarantee or money-back offer
2. **WindPro Mount™ brand** — create a named, tested mounting system as a brand asset
3. **3D solar design visualization** — offer customers a visual of their rooftop before installation
4. **IIT endorsement** — if possible, partner with IISc (Bangalore) for engineering credibility
5. **4.8 Google rating at scale** — 12,000+ reviews means they aggressively collect reviews
6. **"Zero hassle" messaging** — emphasize VSS handles everything (BESCOM, net metering, subsidy)

---

## VSS Threat Assessment
- **Threat level:** 🔴 HIGH
- SolarSquare is the most sophisticated competitor in the market
- Their "Savings Guarantee" is the most powerful trust signal in the industry
- 29 cities means they WILL enter Bangalore more aggressively
- VSS needs to either: (a) match the guarantee, or (b) out-localize them
