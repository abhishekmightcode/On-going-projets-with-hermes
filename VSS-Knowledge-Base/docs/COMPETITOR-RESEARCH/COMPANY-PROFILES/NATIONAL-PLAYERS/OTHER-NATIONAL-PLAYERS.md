# National Solar Players Operating in Bangalore

These are national brands competing in Bangalore alongside local installers:

---

## Tata Power Solar
- **HQ:** Mumbai
- **Strength:** India's largest integrated solar company, part of Tata Group
- **Bangalore presence:** Yes (commercial + industrial focus)
- **Model:** B2B EPC + residential (via dealers)
- **Key differentiator:** Tata brand trust, vertical integration (makes own panels)
- **Margin profile:** 15–22%
- **VSS threat level:** Medium (B2B), Low (residential)

---

## Adani Solar
- **HQ:** Ahmedabad
- **Strength:** Largest Indian solar manufacturer, aggressive pricing
- **Bangalore presence:** Yes (commercial + industrial)
- **Model:** Sell panels + EPC services
- **Key differentiator:** In-house panel manufacturing = lower cost
- **Margin profile:** 12–18%
- **VSS threat level:** Medium (large commercial)

---

## Loom Solar
- **HQ:** Mumbai (originally Maharashtra)
- **Strength:** Strong DTC brand, online presence, YouTube marketing
- **Bangalore presence:** Yes (residential focus)
- **Model:** DTC (sell directly, ship panels, local partner installs)
- **Key differentiator:** Silver iodide mono PERC panels, YouTube brand
- **Website:** www.loomsolar.com
- **VSS threat level:** High (tech-savvy residential customers)

---

## Emmvee Solar
- **HQ:** Bangalore
- **Strength:** Local Bangalore HQ, flexible/film solar panels
- **Bangalore presence:** Strong (local company)
- **Model:** Manufacturer + EPC
- **Key differentiator:** Flexible/film solar panels for irregular rooftops
- **VSS threat level:** High (local advantage)

---

## Vikram Solar
- **HQ:** Kolkata
- **Strength:** EPC focus, large project track record
- **Bangalore presence:** Yes (commercial + industrial)
- **Model:** EPC for large projects
- **Key differentiator:** Utility-scale experience
- **VSS threat level:** Low (residential), Medium (large commercial)

---

## SunPower (Distributed in India via partners)
- **Premium US brand**
- **Target:** Premium residential (high-efficiency panels)
- **Price point:** 30–50% above market
- **VSS threat level:** Low (different segment)

---

## Conclusion

For VSS, the **most dangerous national competitor is Loom Solar** (DTC model, strong brand) and **Emmvee Solar** (local Bangalore HQ with manufacturing). The national giants like Tata and Adani are too large and B2B-focused to directly threaten VSS's residential/SME market.
