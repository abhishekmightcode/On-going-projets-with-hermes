## 🎯 TARGET CUSTOMER PROFILES

### Segment 1: Residential Homeowners
**Profile:**
- Income: ₹8-25 LPA
- House type: Independent house, villa, row house with rooftop
- Monthly electricity bill: ₹3,000 - ₹15,000
- Motivation: Reduce electricity cost, backup power, MNRE subsidy (40-60%)
- Pain points: High upfront cost, confusion about system size, contractor trust

**Typical Journey:**
```
Ad (Google: "solar panel cost Bangalore")
  → Website (calculator used)
  → Lead assigned to field rep
  → Site survey
  → Proposal (system size + savings + EMI)
  → Family decision
  → Installation
  → PMCEDCL/MNRE subsidy claim
  → AMC signup
```

### Segment 2: Commercial & Industrial
**Profile:**
- Factory/warehouse owners, commercial building managers
- Monthly electricity bill: ₹50,000 - ₹10,00,000+
- Motivation: ESG compliance, reduce Opex, duty exemption
- Government mandates (Rooftop solar obligation for Karnataka commercial)
- Timeline: 3-6 months decision cycle

### Segment 3: Housing Societies
**Profile:**
- RWAs (Resident Welfare Associations)
- 50-500 flats per society
- Society secretary as decision maker
- Common rooftop, group buying = lower per-unit cost
- MNRE central sector scheme: 30% subsidy for group housing

### Seasonal Trends
- **Jan-March:** Peak enquiry (summer bills rising)
- **April-June:** Peak installation (sunny season)
- **July-Sep:** Monsoon — reduced installation, maintenance focus
- **Oct-Dec:** Good for housing societies (pre-summer decision)

## 📊 SALES PROCESS (BLUEPRINT)

```
STAGE 1: Lead Generated
  ↓ Lead Source tagged (Google/Facebook/Referral/Walk-in)
  ↓ Locality + System interest captured
  ↓ Assigned to field rep by territory

STAGE 2: Initial Contact
  ↓ Call within 30 mins (SLA)
  ↓ WhatsApp: "Hi {Name}, thanks for your interest in solar!"
  ↓ Qualify: Own house? Monthly bill? Rooftop access?

STAGE 3: Site Survey
  ↓ Schedule site visit (within 48 hrs)
  ↓ Field rep: Check roof area, shadow, structure, electricity meter
  ↓ Survey form filled in CRM (with photos)

STAGE 4: System Design & Proposal
  ↓ System size calculated (based on bill + roof area + budget)
  ↓ Proposal PDF generated (cost, savings, EMI, subsidy amount)
  ↓ Send via WhatsApp + Email

STAGE 5: Proposal Discussion
  ↓ Follow-up call within 48 hrs
  ↓ Handle objections (cost, payback, brand)
  ↓ Share testimonials + installation photos

STAGE 6: Customer Acceptance
  ↓ Booking amount collected (₹10,000-25,000)
  ↓ LOI signed
  ↓ Documentation: Aadhar, electricity bill, property proof

STAGE 7: Installation
  ↓ Installation team scheduled
  ↓ Material delivery tracked
  ↓ Installation completed (1-3 days for residential)
  ↓ Photos uploaded to CRM

STAGE 8: Inspection & Commissioning
  ↓ PMCEDCL/BESCOM inspection
  ↓ Net meter installation
  ↓ System commissioned
  ↓ Performance data connected to monitoring app

STAGE 9: Subsidy Claim
  ↓ MNRE portal application (subsidy form filled)
  ↓ Documents uploaded
  ↓ Subsidy disbursed to bank account (₹50,000-78,000)

STAGE 10: AMC Offer
  ↓ Present AMC plan (₹1,000-3,000/year)
  ↓ If accepted → AMC contract created
  ↓ If not → Mark for future follow-up

STAGE 11: Referral Generation
  ↓ Ask for referrals ("Do you know anyone in your area?")
  ↓ If referral given → New lead created with Referral source
  ↓ Post-installation: 6-month check-in
```
